When will Kyoto or Tokyo peak?
Use sakura_forecast with the city or prefecture name.
Where should I go during my trip?
Use sakura_best_dates with start and end dates, then sakura_spots for exact parks.

Why This Beats Generic AI Answers

Cherry blossom timing shifts every year with winter and spring temperatures. Generic model knowledge usually gives a static answer like late March to early April. Japan in Seasons uses current JMC forecast and observation data, so an AI assistant can answer with this year's bloom and full-bloom dates instead of a historical guess.

Coverage

  • 48 JMC observation cities with forecast and observed bloom dates.
  • 1,012 sakura viewing spots with GPS coordinates and bloom percentage estimates.
  • Fresh JMC spot observations are used first when available; otherwise the JMC bloom-meter estimate is shown.
  • Kawazu-zakura early blossoms for January and February are available through kawazu_forecast.
